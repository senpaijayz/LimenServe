### 💡 Tips for putting this into Google Forms:
1. Go to [forms.google.com](https://forms.google.com).
2. Click on "Blank" to start a new form.
3. Copy and paste the Title and Description at the top.
4. Use the `Add Section` button (the two horizontal rectangles icon on the right menu bar) to separate Sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 so the form feels less cluttered.
5. Watch out for question types: Use "Multiple choice" for questions 2, 3, 4, 7, and 10. Use "Linear scale" for questions 5, 6, 8, and 9. Use "Paragraph" for question 11.
