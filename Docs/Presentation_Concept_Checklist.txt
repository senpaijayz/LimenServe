Title:
LimenServe: A Web-Based Management Information System with Automated Cost Estimation and Quotation for Services and Orders of Limen Auto Parts Center

Researcher/s:
Dela Cruz, Jay Iverson A.
Suacillo, Alden M.
Ulanday, Don Rich M.
Vargas, Christian Dave D.
Villafuerte, Estefhano C.

OBJECTIVES
- To design and develop LimenServe, a web-based MIS that optimizes Limen Auto Parts Center's operations.
- To implement Automated Cost Estimation and Quotation to provide instant, accurate pricing for services and orders.
- To empower customers with a public web portal for real-time stock checks and self-service cost estimation.
- To streamline Service Order Management and POS transactions for improved workflow efficiency.
- To fully digitize all sales records and paper-based transactions into a centralized database.
- To provide a practical, desirable solution with 3D spatial visualization for rapid stock retrieval.
- To ensure project achievability through a reliable, Offline-First architecture.

SCOPE
- User Management: Comprehensive role-based access for Administrators, Stock Clerks, and Cashiers.
- Public Customer Portal: Allows external users to browse inventory and generate cost estimates online (Quotation Only).
- Inventory Module: Real-time tracking of stock levels with low-stock alerts and barcode integration.
- Sales & Payment: Integrated POS system for upfront physical store payments; records all sales to database.
- Automated Cost Estimation & Quotation: Instant calculation and generation of professional quotes for services and parts.
- Service Management: Digital tracking of service orders from intake to completion.
- 3D Spatial Visualization: Digital representation of the stockroom.
- Automated Reporting: Generates sales and inventory reports for business analysis.
- Offline-First Architecture: Ensures reliability during internet outages.

LIMITATIONS
- Cloud Sync Dependency: Remote dashboard requires active internet connectivity.
- Hardware & Platform: PWA optimized for desktop/tablet Chrome browsers; not a native mobile app.
- Scope of Automation: Manual physical placement of items is still required to match 3D coordinates.
- Estimation Accuracy: Estimates are based on standard rates and may differ from final repair needs.
- Transaction Processing: Strict upfront in-store payment policy; no online payment processing.
- Logistics: Supports Metro Manila delivery coordination, but excludes real-time GPS rider/package tracking.
- System Boundary: Single-branch operation; Public Portal is for viewing and quoting only.
